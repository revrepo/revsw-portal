I don't understand my query stats!

Why don't the query stats for all my records add up to the total queries for the zone? This is because we attribute other non-record specific queries to your zone. For example, ANY queries are counted as a single query (they don't count toward every record returned by the query) — so we attribute them to the zone, using the maximum tier of any of the returned records. SOA queries and queries in the zone for records that do not exist are also attributed to the zone as STATIC queries. You may also only see a subset of the records in the zone if your account administrator has restricted your access.
