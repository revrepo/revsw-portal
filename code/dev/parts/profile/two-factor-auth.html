Two-factor authentication (2FA) improves the security of your account. In addition to your normal credentials, you'll also need to provide a one time password (authentication code) when logging in.

Please take the following steps to enable 2FA for your account:

  1. Install Google Authenticator application on your mobile device.
  2. Click on "Set Up Two-Factor Authentication" button below. A QR code will be displayed on the right side of the screen.
  3. In the "Google Authenticator" app add a new account and scan the QR code. The account will be added with label "{{vendorConfig.companyNameShort}}" and your email address. The app will also start displaying one-time six-digit passwords.
  4. To confirm the correctness of the app configuration enter the current six-digit one-time password in the field below and click "Enable 2FA". This step will complete the 2FA activation process.
